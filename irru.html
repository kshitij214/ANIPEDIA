  Predation   

  Currently, predators of black-shouldered opossums are not known. However, a variety of felid species also   occupy their habitat including ocelots, oncillas, margays, jaguars, cougars and jaguarundis, all of which   may prey on black-shouldered opossums. Likewise, the characteristically slow movement of this species   may leave them vulnerable to a variety of predators, specifically when they are juveniles.
