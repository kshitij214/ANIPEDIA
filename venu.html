Glironia venusta(Bushy-tailed opossum)

  Habitat   

Habitat Regions: tropical
Terrestrial Biomes: forest; rainforest
Other Habitat Features: agricultural


  Physical Description   

Physical Features: endothermic; bilateral symmetry
Average mass           Range length
140 g                      355 to 430 mm
4.93 oz                   13.98 to 16.93 in


  Lifespan/Longevity   

  The lifespan of bushy-tailed opossums has not been reported; however, opossums are   generally short-lived, typically living no more than 1 to 2 years.


  Food Habits   

Primary Diet: omnivore
Animal Foods: eggs; insects
Plant Foods: seeds, grains, and nuts; fruit


  Predation   

  Predators of bushy-tailed opossums have not been reported. However, harpy eagles are known to prey on   four-eyed opossums, which share a similar habitat and range. Likewise, their habitat is also occupied by   a variety of felid species including ocelots, oncillas, margays, jaguars, cougars and jaguarundis, all of   which may prey on bushy-tailed opossums.
